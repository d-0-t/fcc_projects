About us

We are a new subdivision of Hum-N Prints LLC, a large company specializing in 3D-printing and growing complex human tissues, the future of organ transplantation.

Our focus is on a very rare type of organ transplantation - bowels and intestines. To synthetize real working digestive tracts would revolutionize the treatment of irreversibly damaged tissues with low risks and improved quality of life.

We are currently working with a research group of doctors and their patients, mostly the ill who have to live with stomas. We're hoping that one day we'll be able to make their life easier and make this technology available for everyone.
